About

Vidyacart – Education Management Platform simplifies the admission process and school commerce for educational institutions. Vidyacart enables Parents to apply for admission to multiple schools / colleges at the click of a button and make online payments with ease. What’s more is the availability of easy EMI facility for fee payment and purchase of school stationary and supplies.

Vidyacart is a one-stop shop for all school and College needs of all categories. Vidyacart makes shopping an alluring experience through hundreds of genuine products to choose from - Uniform, Books, Bags, Stationery, Sports needs etc.. Parents need not hop from shop to shop searching for quality products. Shop online at your comfort from verified vendors, have the products delivered at your convenience with multiple payment options. All products sold on Vidyacart are genuine.
